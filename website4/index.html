SATURNALIA

Saturnalia, one of the largest annual techno-cultural fest of North India, stands as a symbol of pride for Thapar Institute Of Engineering and Technology (TIET), Patiala. It aims to seamlessly integrate various Events, Competitions, Workshops, and a magnificent Star Night, spanning a month . The fest embodies the spirit of creativity, innovation, and camaraderie to allow students to showcase their skills and talents. Join us to celebrate the true spirit of youth and craft a lifetime of cherishes memories and experiences!

Battle of Bands

Battle of Bands is the ultimate platform to showcase your musical prowess and go head-to-head against a curated selection of top-notch bands, representing the pinnacle of musical excellence from renowned colleges.

Singer Night

Saturnalia'23 welcomes the soulful maestro, Mohit Chauhan, headlining Pro Night on November 26. From the fiery 'Khoon Chala' to the yearning 'Tum Se Hi,' his melodies paint emotions on the silver screen. Let's embark on a musical odyssey where his songs, like brushstrokes, color our lives with joy, sorrow, and everything in between.
