Owasp: The Student Chapter

The OWASP student chapter provides an opportunity to the students to work and develop skills in the field of web and mobile applications, and computer information security.

MAIN EVENTS

Capture The Flag

Capture The Flag [CTF] consists of a set of puzzles. or challenges. involving reverse engineering, memory corruption, cryptography, web technologies and more. When participants solve them, they get a "flag", a secret string that can be exchanged for points. The more points a team earns. the higher up it moves in rank.

Hackowasp

A hackathon is like a big brainstorming and building event. People, often with different skills like coding, design, and ideas, come together for a short time, usually a day or a weekend. They work in teams to create new software, apps, or other tech projects. It's a bit like a marathon for creating cool stuff instead of running!
