Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is the purpose of this website?

Answer: This website serves as a comprehensive platform for showcasing all the societies at Thapar University. It provides detailed information about each society, their events, and contact information to help students get involved in campus activities.

Question 2: How can I join a society?

Answer: You can join a society by attending their events and meetings. Each society's contact information is provided on their respective pages. You can reach out to them via email or social media to express your interest in joining.

Question 3: Are there any membership fees for joining a society and Can I join more than one society?

Answer: Membership fees vary from society to society. Some societies may charge a small fee to cover the costs of events and activities, while others may not. Please check the individual society's page for specific details and Yes, you can join multiple societies as long as you can manage your time and commitments effectively. Participating in various societies can enrich your university experience and provide diverse opportunities for growth.

Question 4:How can I stay updated on society events and activities?

Answer: You can stay updated by following the societies on their social media pages and regularly checking this website. Each society's page includes links to their social media accounts where they post updates about upcoming events and activities.

Question 5: Who can I contact for more information?

Answer: For more information, you can reach out to the society representatives listed on their respective pages. Additionally, if you have any general inquiries about the website, you can contact the developers directly using the contact information provided in the footer.

About Us

We have creatd an event website focused on Thapar that features a delightful UI/UX and is highly practical for incoming first-year students. Deploying this will benefit us, especially since it minimizes the need for extensive frontend development beyond HTML and CSS. Using data from various societies, each will have a dedicated page showcasing their events and Instagram feeds. This eliminates the need to scan multiple QR codes during society fairs. Essentially, it consolidates all Thapar societies onto one platform and linking to their respective Instagram pages. This approach not only solves one of issues but also leverages our skills in HTML, CSS, and UI/UX design. It's an ideal project that addresses a practical need within the university community.
