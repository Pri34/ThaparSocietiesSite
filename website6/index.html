FROSH

FROSH WEEK comprises of various activities and events for the fresher class of Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology to ease them into their new surroundings. It includes two major initiatives like PROEM (One week orientation session) and FROSH WEEK (society fair and alumni interactions). The first-year students through these two programs are acclimatised to the facilities at Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology and what the future holds in store for them, not just in their coming four years but also beyond graduation

MAIN EVENTS

ELYSERRA

ElySerra proved to be an enchanting experience, captivating the hearts of newcomers while awakening their creative instincts. This event centred on the witchy spooky realm, seamlessly blending creativity and entertainment.
Society Fair

Society Fair

College is a time to discover yourself, to be on your own, and to begin to fathom life. Students went around over 50 stalls in the fair to look for which clubs or societies ignited their fire of passion. Societies help you feel in charge of yourself and promote your all-round personality development. With the society's own team of seniors presenting on the stall, the students received a strong insight of the society with proper guidance on how to join them and took part in various activities or games that were also organised by the societies themselves
