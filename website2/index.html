SAIC: Student Alumni Interaction Cell

"Student Alumni Interaction Cell, TU, is a platform for the interaction of students and alumni of the Institute, therein helping the students in various aspects, such as training, placements, etc. SAIC organizes various Alumni Reunions, Freshers and farewell functions, Industrial Trips for students and provides corporate networking platforms to students. It also comes up events for the cause of student-development."
